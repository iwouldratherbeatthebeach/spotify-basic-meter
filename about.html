About

Before They Were Basic scans your Spotify liked songs and estimates how obscure or basic each track is using Spotify popularity, artist popularity, and artist follower counts.

It can create Spotify playlists and generate a music taste iceberg meme. It is not affiliated with Spotify.

Spotify does not provide exact play counts through the public Web API, so the app uses popularity-based scoring instead.
