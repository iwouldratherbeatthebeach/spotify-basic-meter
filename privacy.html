Privacy

This MVP runs in your browser. It uses Spotify login to read your saved tracks and create playlists only after you click the relevant buttons.

The app stores your temporary Spotify access token in your browser local storage so the page can call Spotify’s API. Clicking “Clear login” removes it.

This static version does not run a backend database and does not intentionally store your Spotify data on our servers.

Do not use this as final legal language for a public launch. Before marketing this publicly, replace this page with a real privacy policy for your exact deployment.
